About DoWin

Your trusted manufacturing partner from Xiamen, China

Founded in 2008, Xiamen DoWin Industrial & Trade Co., Ltd. is located in Xiamen, Fujian Province — a major port city and hub for export manufacturing in China. We specialize in the design and production of premium leather packaging, gift baskets, and wine hampers.

Our product range includes genuine leather, PU, and PVC materials, serving clients in the jewelry, watch, cosmetics, wine, and gifting industries worldwide.

With a dedicated team of designers, skilled craftsmen, and quality control specialists, DoWin has become a preferred supplier for many international brands and distributors.

Product Features & Materials

Quality materials and craftsmanship in every piece

  • Genuine Leather — Full-grain and top-grain leather for premium products, natural texture and durability
  • PU Leather — Cost-effective alternative with realistic leather appearance, available in 50+ colors
  • PVC Leather — Durable and water-resistant, ideal for high-volume orders and practical packaging
  • Velvet / Suede Linings — Soft interior linings to protect jewelry, watches, and cosmetics
  • Hardware — High-quality zippers, magnetic clasps, and metal accents in gold/silver/nickel finishes
  • Hand-Stitching — Skilled artisans for products requiring hand-sewn details and seams
  • Debossing / Embossing — Custom logo and brand imprint on leather surfaces
  • Hot-Stamping — Foil-stamped logos in gold, silver, or custom colors for luxury finishes
  • Silk-Screen Printing — Precise logo and graphic printing on leather and packaging
  • Laser Engraving — Clean, permanent marking on metal hardware and leather
  • Die-Cut Packaging — Custom-shaped inserts for products (jewelry, watches, bottles)
  • REACH Compliance — All materials tested and certified for restricted substances
  • RoHS Compliant — Hazardous substances eliminated from hardware and finishes
  • Low-Waste Production — Optimized cutting patterns to minimize leather waste
  • FSC-Certified Papers — Packaging papers sourced from responsibly managed forests
  • Water-Based Adhesives — Non-toxic, environmentally safe bonding agents used in assembly
